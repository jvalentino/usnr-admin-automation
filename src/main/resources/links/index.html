Site Purpose Link
NRRM Navy Reserve Readiness Module is a web portal that is restricted to Navy Reserve persons E7 and above. It is where you can access two very important reports:
  1. Smart RUAD- A "Smart" Reserve Unit Assignment Document, which is available in PDF and XLS format. This lists everyone in your unit, their rank/rate, MAS codes, and more.
  2. IMR - An Individual Medical Readiness report for every member of your unit, which details medical information and is available for export in CSV format.
 https://nrrm.navyreserve.navy.mil
SharePoint Contains SharePoint pages for every Navy Reserve unit by function and region, as well as links to other Navy websites. https://private.navyreserve.navy.mil
NROWS The Navy Reserve Order Writing System is what you use for any type type of orders. Note that you use your email certificate and must be using Internet Explorer. https://nrows.sscno.nmci.navy.mil/nrows
DFAS The Defense Finance and Accounting Service is a web portal where you can get pay and tax information (like your W2) https://mypay.dfas.mil/mypay.aspx
NFAAS Navy Family Accountability and Assessment System is a web portal that is used to track your personal information in case of a recall. You generally have to go here several times a year to verify your information. https://navyfamily.navy.mil/
BUPERS United States Navy's Bureau of Naval Personnel https://www.bol.navy.mil/
JOAPPLY Site for browsing and applying for JO Billets https://private.navyreserve.navy.mil/Apply/
NSIPS The Navy Standard Integrated Personnel System (NSIPS) is the Navy’s single, field-entry, electronic pay and personnel system for all USN/USNR Sailors https://nsipsprod.nmci.navy.mil/nsipsclo/jsp/index.jsp
milConnect milConnect is a web application provided by the DMDC that offers sponsors, spouses, and their children (18 years and older) access to their personal information https://milconnect.dmdc.mil/
DTS The Defense Travel System (DTS) is a fully integrated, automated, end-to-end travel management system that enables DoD travelers to create authorizations (TDY travel orders), prepare reservations, receive approvals, generate travel vouchers, and receive a split reimbursement between their bank accounts and the Government Travel Charge Card (GTCC) vendor https://www.defensetravel.osd.mil/
e-QIP Electronic Questionnaires for Investigations Processing http://www.opm.gov/e-qip/
